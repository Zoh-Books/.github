# Zoho Books – Smart Accounting & Financial Management Tool

**Zoho Books** is a powerful, flexible accounting solution designed to help businesses organize finances, track transactions, and achieve tax compliance.  
With **Zoho Books**, you can transform chaotic receipts into structured ledgers.  
**Zoho Books** supports automated invoicing, expense tracking, and bank reconciliation, making it an essential financial companion for entrepreneurs, accountants, and small business owners.

---

## About Zoho Books

**Zoho Books** combines the simplicity of a checkbook with the power of an ERP system. Unlike basic accounting tools, **Zoho Books** offers deep customization: chart of accounts, custom fields, approval workflows, and recurring bills.  
**Zoho Books** works seamlessly with your existing bank feeds (Plaid, Yodlee, and direct CSV import) and provides real-time updates across all devices.  
Whether you manage freelance income or retail inventory, **Zoho Books** adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.  
**Zoho Books** respects your privacy – all financial data stays under your control with role-based access and optional audit logs.  
With **Zoho Books**, you gain clarity, reduce manual entry errors, and close books faster every month.

## Key Features of Zoho Books

- **Automated Invoicing** – **Zoho Books** sends payment reminders and late fees automatically.  
- **Bank Reconciliation** – **Zoho Books** matches imported transactions with ledger entries.  
- **Expense Tracking** – Scan receipts and **Zoho Books** extracts data via OCR.  
- **Tax Compliance** – **Zoho Books** calculates GST, VAT, or sales tax per jurisdiction.  
- **Purchase Orders** – Create and track POs inside **Zoho Books** with approval stages.  
- **Vendor Payments** – Schedule payments and record 1099 contractor info in **Zoho Books**.  
- **Inventory Management** – **Zoho Books** adjusts stock levels automatically on invoice creation.  
- **Multi-Currency** – **Zoho Books** converts foreign transactions using daily exchange rates.  
- **Financial Reports** – Generate P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow inside **Zoho Books**.  
- **Audit Trail** – **Zoho Books** logs every create, edit, and delete action.  
- **Project Billing** – Track billable hours and expenses per project in **Zoho Books**.  
- **Recurring Bills** – Set weekly or monthly vendor bills inside **Zoho Books**.  
- **Attachments** – Store receipts, contracts, and statements inside **Zoho Books**.  
- **Role-Based Access** – Assign view/edit permissions in **Zoho Books** for team members.  
- **Export & Backup** – Export your **Zoho Books** data to Excel, PDF, or JSON.
